Raven's mission is Operation Cinder—a covert campaign to dismantle the dictatorship in Kuamar without triggering an open war. Instead of invading the country, his employers decide to eliminate the regime's key leaders one by one through precision sniper operations. 

Who is Raven?

Raven is an American freelance contract sniper and assassin. He is not an active-duty soldier; he's an independent operative with experience working alongside organizations such as U.S. Special Forces, the FBI, and the British SAS. During the operation, he is guided remotely by his handler, Alex ("Control"). 

Who sent him?

The game deliberately keeps Raven's employer anonymous.

Raven is hired by an unknown client operating through a covert organization. Alex acts as Raven's mission controller, relaying intelligence, assigning contracts, and coordinating the operation, but the identity of the ultimate sponsor is never revealed. This ambiguity reinforces the idea that the mission is meant to be deniable—if Raven is caught, no government officially acknowledges him. 

Raven's objectives

His mission unfolds in stages:

1. Cripple Kuamar's military by eliminating generals, commanders, and officers.


2. Destroy strategic assets, including weapons programs and infrastructure.


3. Disrupt the regime's intelligence and security apparatus.


4. Prevent Kuamar from escalating into a wider regional conflict.


5. Assassinate President Bibi Rashida and other senior leaders, bringing down the dictatorship. 



The campaign is essentially a decapitation strike: instead of fighting thousands of enemy soldiers, Raven removes the people whose leadership keeps the regime functioning. Each completed contract weakens Kuamar's ability to govern and wage war until the leadership structure begins to collapse.
