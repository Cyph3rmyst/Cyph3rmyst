The actual licence ladder

Regardless of the pathway, the licences usually follow this order:

Medical

↓

Student Pilot Licence

↓

Private Pilot Licence

↓

Commercial Pilot Licence

↓

Instrument Rating

↓

Multi Engine Rating

↓

Type Rating

↓

Airline Transport Pilot Licence

KCAA requires, among other things:

Student Pilot Licence: minimum age 16, Class 2 medical.

Private Pilot Licence: minimum age 17, Class 2 medical.

Commercial Pilot Licence: minimum age 18, Class 1 medical, PPL, required flight experience, knowledge and practical tests, and English language proficiency.

Airline Transport Pilot Licence: minimum age 21, Class 1 medical, CPL with additional experience and examinations.
