## PROLOGUE: AFTER RAVEN

The world remembers **Raven** as the ghost who ended the reign of President **Bibi Rashida** and dismantled the machinery that kept Kuamar under control. The sniper's rifle went silent after the final contract. The leadership fell. The regime collapsed.

But wars do not always end when the last target falls.

When Rashida's government disappeared, a new battlefield emerged beneath the surface.

The physical battlefields of Kuamar were abandoned, but its **digital infrastructure remained alive**:

* Military networks still carried forgotten commands.
* Government databases contained secrets from the old regime.
* Industrial systems controlling energy and water remained exposed.
* Intelligence archives became targets for foreign actors.
* Criminal groups and rogue operators began exploiting the chaos.

Kuamar had survived a political collapse.

Now it faced a **cyber occupation**.

The world needed a different kind of operator.

Not a soldier.

Not a sniper.

A cyber warrior.
