Here's a solid list to bookmark. I would split them into four folders in your browser.

🇰🇪 1. Kenya (check these regularly)

[Kenya Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA) – Approved Training Organizations](https://www.kcaa.or.ke/personnel-licencing/approved-training-organization?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

The official list of KCAA-approved flight schools. This should be your first stop when you're ready to begin self-sponsored training. 


[Kenya Airways – Flying Schools in Kenya](https://www.kenya-airways.ke/flying-schools-in-kenya/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

Good overview of becoming a pilot and links to approved schools. 


[East African School of Aviation (EASA)](https://www.kcaa.or.ke/easa/about-easa?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

Offers aviation courses and is a major aviation training institution in Kenya.

📅 A simple routine while you're in campus

Once a month (it only takes about 20–30 minutes):

Check the KCAA approved training list.

Check Kenya Airways Careers.

Check Emirates Careers.

Check Qatar Airways Careers.

Check Ethiopian Airlines Careers.

Search for "pilot cadet programme" on Google or LinkedIn.


This keeps you aware of opportunities without distracting from your IT studies.

Since self-sponsored is your primary plan, think of these websites as lottery tickets you earn by applying, not as your only route. If one opportunity opens, great. If none do, you're still progressing toward the same goal through self-funded training. 🚁✈️
