Is it worth it?

For someone planning to self-sponsor, Kenya School of Flying is considered one of Kenya's premier flight schools, but it is also among the more expensive options. Many students compare it with schools like Flight Training Centre (FTC), Ninety Nines Flying School, and some South African academies before making a decision.
