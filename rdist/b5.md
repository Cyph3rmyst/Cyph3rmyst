Raven's operation takes him across five major regions of Kuamar (plus a tutorial mission). Each region represents a different strategic part of the country and serves a specific purpose in dismantling the Rashida regime. 

1. Grey Desert (Tutorial)

This is Raven's introduction to Kuamar. It serves as a training area where Alex teaches the fundamentals of reconnaissance, sniping, and infiltration before Raven begins the real contracts. 


---

2. Zindah Province

This is a coastal industrial region centered around a busy port.

Key locations include:

Shipping docks

Cargo container yards

Oil and fuel storage facilities

Lighthouse

Military checkpoints

Industrial warehouses


Here, Raven targets military logistics and supply operations. Many of the game's iconic 1,000+ meter shots occur in this region, where he eliminates high-value targets from distant cliffs overlooking the port. 


---

3. Mount Kuamar

This is a rugged mountain stronghold that houses one of the regime's most secure installations.

Key locations include:

The Citadel bunker

Satellite communications array

Water pumping station

Underground tunnel network

Radar and jamming facilities

Fortified research complex


One of Raven's primary objectives here is eliminating Lars Hellström, a hacker and cyber warfare specialist working for the regime. He also sabotages communications and uploads a virus into the Citadel's mainframe. 


---

4. Tajmid Heights

This is a mountainous frontier region filled with military installations.

Key locations include:

Missile bases

Radar stations

Border forts

Military camps

Cliffside sniper positions

Desert valleys


The focus here is weakening Kuamar's military command and preventing the regime from launching a larger regional conflict. Raven systematically removes senior officers and disables strategic military assets. 


---

5. Rashida Qalat

This is the regime's capital region and the final area of the campaign.

Key locations include:

Presidential palace

Government compounds

Luxury villas

Military headquarters

Defensive walls and towers

Elite guard positions


This is the climax of the game, where Raven penetrates the regime's political heart to eliminate its remaining leadership and complete Operation Cinder. 

How these regions fit together

Rather than moving randomly through Kuamar, Raven's campaign follows a logical progression:

1. Grey Desert – Training and insertion.


2. Zindah Province – Cripple logistics and supply lines.


3. Mount Kuamar – Destroy communications and cyber capabilities.


4. Tajmid Heights – Neutralize military command and strategic defenses.


5. Rashida Qalat – Strike the regime's leadership and end the operation.



Together, these regions portray Kuamar as a country with diverse terrain—coastal industrial zones, deserts, mountains, border fortifications, and a heavily defended political center—each contributing to the regime's ability to maintain power.
