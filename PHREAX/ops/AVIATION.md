# ✈️ AVIATION PATH — THE PROCESS OF BECOMING

> *"Don't replace YouTube. Replace passive consumption with purposeful learning."*

---

## 🌅 Why This Exists

I realized that when I'm not doing cybersecurity work, I often default to binge-watching random YouTube videos.

The solution isn't to stop watching YouTube.

The solution is to transform YouTube into a university.

Aviation has been a lifelong dream of mine. Instead of consuming content that leaves nothing behind, I will use that same time to build knowledge that compounds over the years.

Cybersecurity remains my primary mission.

Aviation becomes my purposeful recovery—a different discipline that exercises another part of my mind while keeping me moving toward the person I want to become.

This isn't a distraction.

It's another path in **the process of becoming**.

---

# 🛫 Learning Philosophy

Learn aviation exactly the way I learn cybersecurity.

* Learn from first principles.
* Take notes.
* Build understanding before memorization.
* Document everything.
* Learn consistently.
* Enjoy the journey.

The goal is not to rush toward a pilot license.

The goal is to become the kind of person who thinks like a pilot long before sitting in the left seat.
