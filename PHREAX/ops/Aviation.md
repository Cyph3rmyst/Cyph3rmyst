# ✈️ AVIATION

> *"The route changed. The destination didn't."*

## About

Aviation has been a lifelong dream of mine.

When I was four years old, I watched a Cessna aircraft flying low over my home during a wildlife search operation. That single moment sparked a fascination that has stayed with me ever since.

Although I pursued an IT degree and am specializing in cybersecurity, aviation has never left my mind.

Today, cybersecurity is the career I am building.

Aviation remains a lifelong passion that I continue to study one lesson at a time.

---

# Purpose

This repository exists to document my aviation learning journey.

It is not intended to replace formal flight training.

Instead, it serves as a structured place to learn aviation theory, understand how aircraft and pilots operate, and prepare myself for the day I eventually attend flight school.

Rather than spending countless hours passively scrolling social media or binge-watching random videos, I choose to invest that time learning something that has inspired me since childhood.

Every note is another step toward becoming a more knowledgeable and capable person.

---

# Philosophy

I believe learning should be documented.

This repository is not about appearing knowledgeable.

It is about preserving the process of learning.

The objective is simple:

Learn deeply.

Think critically.

Document consistently.

Repeat.
